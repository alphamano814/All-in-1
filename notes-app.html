About This Notes App

Our Online Notes App is a free tool that helps you create, save, and manage your notes directly in your browser. All your notes are stored locally, so they're private and secure.

How to Use the Notes App

Using our notes app is simple:

  • Click the + button to create a new note
  • Add a title and content to your note
  • Click the pin icon to pin important notes to the top
  • Use the search bar to find specific notes
  • Edit notes by clicking on the title or content
  • Delete notes with the trash icon
  • Copy note content with the copy icon

Key Features

This free notes app includes all the essential features you need:

  • Create, edit, and delete notes
  • Auto-save to browser storage
  • Pin important notes
  • Search through all your notes
  • Word and character count
  • Dark and light mode
  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Copy note content to clipboard

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my notes stored?

All your notes are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means your notes are private and secure, but they will be lost if you clear your browser data.

Can I access my notes on different devices?

No, since notes are stored locally in your browser, they are only available on the device where you created them. For cross-device sync, you would need a cloud-based notes app.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our Online Notes App is completely free to use with no registration required. You can use it as many times as you need without any limitations.

How do I export my notes?

Currently, you can copy individual notes to your clipboard using the copy button. We're working on adding export functionality for all notes in future updates.
