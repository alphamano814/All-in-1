How to Use the Tip Calculator

  1. Enter Bill Amount: Input the total bill amount before tip in the first field.
  2. Select Tip Percentage: Choose from preset tip percentages (5%, 10%, 15%, 20%) or enter a custom percentage.
  3. Specify Number of People: Enter how many people are splitting the bill (default is 1 for individual calculation).
  4. Calculate: Click "Calculate Tip" or let the tool automatically update as you enter values.
  5. View Results: Check the results section for detailed breakdown including tip per person, total tip, total bill, and total per person.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the standard tip percentage?
The standard tip percentage varies by country and service type. In the United States, 15-20% is generally considered standard for restaurant service, with 15% for good service and 20% for excellent service.
Should I tip on tax?
Generally, it's recommended to calculate tip based on the pre-tax amount. However, some people prefer to tip on the total including tax. Our calculator allows you to enter whichever amount you prefer.
How do I calculate tip for bad service?
For subpar service, you might consider a lower tip percentage (10% or less) or speaking with management about the service issues. The tip should reflect the quality of service received.
Is this tip calculator free to use?
Yes, our tip calculator is completely free to use with no limitations. You can use it as often as you need without any registration or subscription.
