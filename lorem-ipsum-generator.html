Professional Lorem Ipsum Generator for Designers & Developers

Our free online Lorem Ipsum Generator helps designers, developers, and content creators generate placeholder text for mockups, prototypes, and design layouts. Create custom text in multiple styles including classic Latin, modern hipster, technology-focused, and more.

All text generation happens locally in your browser - no data is sent to external servers, ensuring complete privacy and instant generation speeds for your projects.

How to Use the Lorem Ipsum Generator

  1. Select output type - Choose between paragraphs, sentences, words, or bytes
  2. Set quantity - Specify how much text you need (1-200 paragraphs, 1-1000 sentences, etc.)
  3. Choose language/style - Pick from Classic Latin, Short, Hipster, Tech, or create your own Custom wordlist
  4. Configure options - Enable "Start with Lorem ipsum" and include punctuation as needed
  5. Generate text - Click the Generate button or use Live Mode for real-time updates
  6. Copy or download - Use the Copy button for clipboard or Download for a text file

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lorem Ipsum and why is it used?
Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text commonly used in the design industry to demonstrate the visual form of documents or websites without relying on meaningful content. It helps designers focus on layout and design elements.
Can I use custom words for the generated text?
Yes! Our generator includes a Custom Wordlist option where you can provide your own words separated by commas. The generator will use these words to create the placeholder text.
What's the difference between the different language styles?
Classic Latin uses traditional Lorem Ipsum words, Short style uses concise modern words, Hipster includes trendy modern terms, and Tech focuses on technology-related vocabulary. Each serves different design contexts.
