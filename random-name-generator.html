Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Name Generator work?

Our name generator uses a curated database of names from various cultures and styles. When you select your preferences, it filters the database and randomly selects names that match your criteria.

Can I save names for later?

Yes! You can click the star icon on any generated name to add it to your favorites. Your favorites are saved in your browser's local storage, so they'll be there when you return.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our Random Name Generator is completely free to use with no registration required. You can use it as many times as you need without any limitations.

Can I suggest new names or origins?

We're always expanding our name database. If you have suggestions for names or origins we should include, please contact us through our website.
