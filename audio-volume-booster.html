Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Audio Volume Booster work?

Our tool uses the Web Audio API to process your audio files directly in your browser. When you adjust the volume slider, it modifies the gain level of the audio signal, effectively increasing or decreasing the volume without requiring any server processing.

What audio formats are supported?

The tool supports most common audio formats including MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A, and AAC. The browser's capabilities determine which formats can be processed, but most modern browsers support these formats.

Is there a file size limit?

There's no hard limit, but very large files may take longer to process and could impact browser performance. For best results, we recommend files under 100MB.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our Audio Volume Booster is completely free to use with no registration required. You can use it as many times as you need without any limitations.

Will boosting volume affect audio quality?

Moderate volume boosting (up to 150%) typically preserves audio quality well. Higher boosts may introduce some distortion, especially if the original audio was already at a high volume. The tool includes safeguards to minimize quality loss.

Can I use this tool on mobile devices?

Yes, the tool is fully responsive and works on most modern mobile browsers. However, processing very large files on mobile devices may be slower than on desktop computers.
