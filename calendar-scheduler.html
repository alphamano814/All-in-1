About This Calendar Scheduler

Our Online Calendar Scheduler is a free tool that helps you plan, organize, and track your events directly in your browser. With a clean, intuitive interface, you can easily manage your schedule without any registration or installation required.

How to Use the Calendar Scheduler

Using our calendar scheduler is simple:

  • Navigate: Use the Previous and Next buttons to browse through months
  • Add Events: Click on any date to add a new event
  • View Events: Click on event dots to view and edit event details
  • Search: Use the search bar to find events by title or description
  • Reset: Use the "Reset All" button to clear all events (with confirmation)

Key Features

This free calendar scheduler offers powerful features to help you stay organized:

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Calendar Scheduler save my events?

All your events are saved directly in your browser's localStorage. This means your data stays on your device and doesn't require any account or internet connection after the initial page load.

Can I access my calendar from multiple devices?

No, since the data is stored locally in your browser, it's only accessible from the same device and browser where you created the events. For multi-device access, consider using a cloud-based calendar service.

What happens if I clear my browser data?

If you clear your browser's cache and localStorage, all your events will be permanently deleted. We recommend exporting important events regularly if you need to keep backups.

Is there a limit to how many events I can create?

There's no hard limit, but browser localStorage typically has a storage limit of 5-10MB depending on the browser, which should be more than enough for thousands of events.
