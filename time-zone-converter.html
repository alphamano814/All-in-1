How to Use the Time Zone Converter

  1. Enter Time: Input the time you want to convert using the time selector.
  2. Select Date (Optional): Choose a specific date if needed, or leave blank to use today's date.
  3. Choose Time Zones: Select your source time zone and target time zone from the dropdown menus.
  4. Convert: Click the "Convert Time" button or let the tool automatically update as you make changes.
  5. Swap Time Zones: Use the swap button to quickly reverse your source and target time zones.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between UTC and GMT?
UTC (Coordinated Universal Time) is the primary time standard while GMT (Greenwich Mean Time) is a time zone. For most practical purposes, they are equivalent and have the same time offset (UTC+0).
Do you account for Daylight Saving Time?
Yes, our time zone converter includes both standard and daylight saving time variants for regions that observe DST, such as EST/EDT, PST/PDT, etc.
How accurate is the time zone conversion?
Our conversions are mathematically precise based on the UTC offsets of each time zone. The tool accounts for the exact time difference between zones down to the minute.
Is this time zone converter free to use?
Yes, our time zone converter is completely free to use with no limitations. You can use it as often as you need without any registration or subscription.
