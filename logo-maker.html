Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Logo Maker really free?

Yes, our logo maker is completely free to use with no registration required. You can create and download as many logos as you want without any limitations.

What file formats can I export my logo in?

You can export your logo in both PNG and SVG formats. PNG is great for web use, while SVG is a vector format that can be scaled to any size without losing quality.

Can I use the logos I create for commercial purposes?

Yes, you have full rights to any logos you create with our tool. They are yours to use for personal or commercial projects without any restrictions.

Do I need to create an account to use the Logo Maker?

No account is required. Our logo maker works entirely in your browser, and you can save your projects locally if needed.

Can I edit my logo after saving the project?

Yes, you can save your project and load it later to continue editing. Your projects are saved in your browser's local storage.
