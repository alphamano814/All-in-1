About This Age Calculator

Our Online Age Calculator is a free tool that helps you determine your exact age in years, months, and days. Whether you're calculating your own age, a child's age, or someone else's, this tool provides accurate results instantly.

How to Use the Age Calculator

Using our age calculator is simple:

  • Enter your date of birth in the first field
  • Optionally select a specific date to calculate your age on that date (defaults to today)
  • Click the "Calculate Age" button
  • View your exact age in years, months, and days

What This Tool Does

This free age calculator tool accurately calculates the time elapsed between your date of birth and the selected date. It's perfect for:

  • Determining your exact age for official documents
  • Calculating children's ages for school enrollment
  • Planning birthday celebrations and anniversaries
  • Tracking development milestones
  • Verifying age for legal purposes

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Age Calculator work?

Our age calculator calculates the difference between your date of birth and the selected date, accounting for varying month lengths and leap years. It provides an accurate breakdown in years, months, and days.

Can I calculate age in months and days only?

Yes, the calculator shows your complete age breakdown including years, months, and days. For infants and young children, the month and day calculation is particularly useful for tracking development.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our Online Age Calculator is completely free to use with no registration required. You can use it as many times as you need without any limitations.

Can I calculate age for a future date?

No, the calculator only works for dates up to the current date or a past date. It cannot calculate age for future dates as that would require predicting the future.
