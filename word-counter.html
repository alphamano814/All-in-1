Free Online Word Counter Tool

Our Word Counter is a free online tool that helps you analyze your text by counting words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, and paragraphs. Whether you're a writer, student, or professional, this tool provides accurate statistics to help you meet specific word count requirements or analyze your writing patterns.

With real-time updates as you type, our word counter helps you track your progress without interrupting your workflow. The tool works with any type of text - from short social media posts to lengthy academic papers.

How to Use the Word Counter

  1. Enter your text - Type or paste your text into the text area above
  2. View real-time statistics - As you type, the word count, character count, sentence count, and paragraph count will update automatically
  3. Analyze your text - Review the statistics to understand the composition of your text
  4. Reset when needed - Use the reset button to clear the text area and start over with new text

The tool automatically calculates all metrics in real-time, so you don't need to click any "count" button - just start typing!

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the word counter handle hyphenated words?

Our word counter treats hyphenated words (like "state-of-the-art") as single words. This follows standard word counting conventions used by most writing platforms and publishers.

Does the character count include punctuation?

Yes, both character counts (with and without spaces) include all punctuation marks. The "characters with spaces" count includes spaces between words, while "characters without spaces" excludes them.

How are sentences counted?

Sentences are identified by common sentence-ending punctuation: periods, exclamation marks, and question marks. Each group of text ending with one of these punctuation marks is counted as a sentence.

Is there a limit to how much text I can analyze?

No, there's no strict limit to the amount of text you can analyze. However, extremely long documents may experience slight performance delays as the tool processes the text in real-time.
