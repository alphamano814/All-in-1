Which format should I choose for my images?
  • JPEG: Best for photographs with many colors
  • PNG: Best for images with transparency or sharp edges
  • WebP: Modern format with excellent compression
  • GIF: For simple animations
  • BMP: Uncompressed format for maximum quality
