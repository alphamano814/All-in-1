About This URL Shortener

Our URL Shortener is a free browser-based tool that lets you shorten long URLs, track clicks, and manage your links without any server involvement. All data is stored locally in your browser for maximum privacy.

How to Use the URL Shortener

Using our URL shortener is simple:

  • Enter the long URL you want to shorten in the input field
  • Optionally specify a custom alias for your short URL
  • Click "Shorten URL" to generate your short link
  • Copy the short URL or generate a QR code for it
  • Track clicks and manage your links in the history section

Key Features

This free URL shortener offers powerful features for managing your links:

  • 100% client-side processing - your URLs never leave your browser

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my shortened URLs stored?

All your shortened URLs and their data are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This means your data stays on your device and is not uploaded to any server.

Can I use these short URLs on other devices?

No, since the data is stored locally in your browser, the short URLs will only work on the same device and browser where you created them. For cross-device URL shortening, consider using a cloud-based service.

What happens if I clear my browser data?

If you clear your browser's cache and localStorage, all your shortened URLs will be permanently deleted. We recommend exporting important links regularly if you need to keep backups.

Is there a limit to how many URLs I can shorten?

There's no specific limit, but browser localStorage typically has a storage limit of 5-10MB depending on the browser, which should be more than enough for thousands of URLs.
