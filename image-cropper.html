About This Image Cropper

Our Free Image Cropper is a powerful online tool that allows you to crop and resize images with precision. Whether you need to crop photos for social media, create profile pictures, or prepare images for your website, this tool makes it easy and efficient.

How to Use the Image Cropper

Cropping your image is simple:

  • Upload your image by dragging and dropping or using the file browser
  • Adjust the crop area by dragging the corners or edges
  • Use the aspect ratio controls to maintain specific proportions
  • Rotate or zoom the image if needed
  • Preview your cropped image in different aspect ratios
  • Download the result in JPG or PNG format

Key Features

Our image cropper offers a comprehensive set of features:

  • Precise Cropping: Drag to select exactly the area you want to keep
  • Aspect Ratio Control: Maintain specific proportions like square (1:1), widescreen (16:9), or portrait (3:4)
  • Rotation: Rotate your image to the perfect angle
  • Zoom: Get a closer look at details for precise cropping
  • Live Preview: See how your cropped image will look in different formats
  • Multiple Formats: Download in JPG or PNG format

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats can I crop with this tool?

You can upload JPG, PNG, and WEBP images. The tool will process them in your browser and allow you to download the cropped result in either JPG or PNG format.

Is there a file size limit for uploaded images?

The maximum file size for uploads is 10MB. For best results, we recommend using images that are at least 100×100 pixels.

Can I crop images to specific dimensions?

Yes, you can set specific aspect ratios or use the freeform cropping option. The tool shows the dimensions of your crop area as you adjust it.

Does the image cropper work on mobile devices?

Yes, our image cropper is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. The touch-friendly interface makes it easy to crop images on mobile.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.
