Free Online Discount Calculator

Our free online discount calculator helps you quickly determine the final price after applying single or multiple discounts. Whether you're shopping, setting prices for your business, or planning your budget, this tool provides accurate calculations with detailed breakdowns.

Simply enter the original price, apply discounts as percentages or fixed amounts, add tax if needed, and see exactly how much you'll save. Perfect for shoppers, business owners, students, and anyone who wants to make informed purchasing decisions.

How to Use the Discount Calculator

Our discount calculator is designed to be intuitive and easy to use. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Enter the original price - Input the initial price of the item before any discounts.
  2. Set your first discount - Choose between percentage or fixed amount discount and enter the value.
  3. Add additional discounts (optional) - Check the "Apply Additional Discount" box to add a second discount.
  4. Include tax (optional) - If you need to calculate the final price including tax, check the "Include Sales Tax" box and enter the tax rate.
  5. Adjust quantity - Enter the number of items you're purchasing to see the total price.
  6. Select currency - Choose your preferred currency from the dropdown menu.
  7. Calculate - Click the "Calculate Discount" button to see your results instantly.

The calculator will show you the discounted price per item, total price for all items, total savings amount, and savings percentage. You'll also get a detailed breakdown of how the final price was calculated.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate multiple discounts?
To calculate multiple discounts, simply check the "Apply Additional Discount" box after setting your first discount. The calculator will apply the discounts sequentially and show you the final price after all discounts.
What's the difference between percentage and fixed amount discounts?
A percentage discount reduces the price by a certain percentage (e.g., 20% off), while a fixed amount discount subtracts a specific amount from the price (e.g., $10 off). The calculator supports both types and allows you to mix them.
Can I calculate the final price including tax?
Yes, you can! Check the "Include Sales Tax" box and enter the tax rate. The calculator will add the tax to the discounted price to show you the final amount you'll pay.
Is this discount calculator completely free?
Absolutely! Our discount calculator is completely free to use with no hidden costs or registration requirements. We believe in providing useful tools to help everyone make informed financial decisions.
