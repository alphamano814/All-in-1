How to Use the Image Compressor

Our image compressor is designed to be intuitive and easy to use. Follow these simple steps to compress your images:

  1. Upload your images - Drag and drop images into the upload area or click to browse your files. You can upload multiple images at once.
  2. Configure compression settings - Choose your preferred output format, quality level, and resize options. Use the presets for quick sizing.
  3. Apply advanced options (optional) - For more control, expand the advanced options to adjust chroma subsampling or color reduction.
  4. Compress images - Click the "Compress All Images" button to start the compression process.
  5. Download results - Once compression is complete, download individual images or all images as a ZIP file.

All processing happens locally in your browser, so your images never leave your computer. This ensures complete privacy and security for your sensitive images.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my image data secure?
Absolutely! All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.
What image formats are supported?
Our tool supports PNG, JPG, JPEG, WebP, and GIF formats. You can compress images in their original format or convert them to JPEG, PNG, or WebP.
How much can I reduce image file sizes?
Compression results vary depending on the original image and settings. Typically, you can achieve 50-80% reduction in file size while maintaining good visual quality.
Is there a limit to how many images I can compress?
No, there are no limits! You can compress as many images as you want, completely free. The only limitation is your device's memory and processing power.
What's the difference between JPEG and WebP format?
WebP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior compression compared to JPEG. WebP images are typically 25-35% smaller than JPEGs at equivalent quality.
